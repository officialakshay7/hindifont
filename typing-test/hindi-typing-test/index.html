Frequently Asked Questions

WPM = Words Per Minute। एक word = 5 characters। 25 WPM = 1 minute में 125 characters type करना।
SSC: 25-35 WPM | CPCT: 5000 KDPH | UP Police: 25 WPM | Railway: 25 WPM। Official notification check करें।
